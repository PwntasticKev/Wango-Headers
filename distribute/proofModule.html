New Distilleries!
Our mission has always been to celebrate and promote the diverse spirits industry within Portland and as the number of distilleries in PDX has grown, so too has the scope and geography of Distillery Row.
Hence, we are excited to announce that Distillery Row has expanded to the west side!

Aria Dry Portland Gin, Bull Run Distillery, and Freeland Spirits are now official members of Distillery Row. These new members consist of long-time local distillers (several with roots as original founders of Distillery Row) as well as new creators in the field. We are excited to absorb them into the organization and look forward to introducing locals and visitors alike to their amazing craft spirits, cocktails, and events.


But that's not all! The latest addition to the scene is Straightaway Spirits. Located in the heart of SE, Straightaway represents the latest evolution in the craft spirits industry--bottle-ready cocktails! Visit their beautiful new tasting room right off Hawthorne Blvd.

New Features!

The 2019 Distillery Row Passport program now offers more benefits and opportunities to experience, learn, and share in the unique destination that is Distillery Row. Your passport is now your VIP membership to events and partnerships all year long.
  • $5 credit toward tasting fees at each distillery (12 distilleries + 2 second locations = $70 value)*
  • Access to exclusive Distillery Row cocktails at partnering restaurants and bars
  • Special discounts and savings at local businesses
  • Advanced invitation to new product launches
  • Discounts on select distillery events & classes
Visit the Exclusive Benefits page on this site for current partnerships and discounts. Or order the mobile passport and have them instantly update to your account!
